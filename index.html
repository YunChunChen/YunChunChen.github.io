About me

I am a short-term visiting scholar in the Vision and Learning Lab at University of California, Merced under the supervision of Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. Previously, I am fortunate to work with Prof. Jia-Bin Huang (Virginia Tech), Prof. Yen-Yu Lin (Academia Sinica), Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang (NTUEE), and Prof. Winston H. Hsu (NTUCSIE).

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision and deep learning. I'm particularly interested in Unsupervised Domain Adaptation, Meta-Learning, and Representation Learning.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University in 2018.
