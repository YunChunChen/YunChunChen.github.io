About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Toronto, advised by Animesh Garg. I also collaborate with Alec Jacobson. I am a student researcher at the Vector Institute and a member of the UofT Robotics Institute and the UofT Data Sciences Institute.

I also work as a research intern at NVIDIA Research on the Robotics team, working with Dieter Fox, Adithya Murali and Balakumar Sundaralingam.

My research interests are in the areas of computer vision and robotics. I am particularly interested in representation learning, geometric deep learning, and 3D vision.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Electrical Engineering from National Taiwan University in 2018. Previously, I was fortunate to work with Ming-Hsuan Yang at UC Merced, Jia-Bin Huang at Virginia Tech, and Yen-Yu Lin at Academia Sinica.
