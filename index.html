About me

I am a short-term visiting scholar in the Vision and Learning Lab at University of California at Merced under the supervision of Prof. Ming-Hsuan Yang. Previously, I am fortunate to work with Prof. Jia-Bin Huang (Virginia Tech), Prof. Yen-Yu Lin (Academia Sinica), and Prof. Winston Hsu (NTU).

My research interests lie in the intersection of computer vision and deep learning. I'm particularly interested in Unsupervised Domain Adaptation, Meta-Learning, and Representation Learning.

I received my Bachelor of Science degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University in 2018.

I will start my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Toronto in Fall 2020.
